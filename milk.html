🥛 Why Cattle Health & Milk Yield Monitoring Matters

Cattle health directly influences milk yield, farm profitability, and product quality. By continuously tracking vital health indicators like temperature, feed intake, and activity levels, dairy farmers can detect early signs of illness, optimize feeding schedules, and enhance overall productivity. This ensures animal welfare, reduces losses, and supports sustainable dairy practices.

📈 Benefits of AI-Based Dairy Monitoring

  • Improves overall milk yield and quality
  • Enables early detection of cattle health issues
  • Optimizes feeding, breeding, and milking schedules
  • Reduces veterinary costs through preventive care
  • Ensures better animal welfare and farm efficiency
