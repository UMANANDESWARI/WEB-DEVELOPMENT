🌱 Why Soil Quality Detection Matters

Soil quality affects everything from crop yield to environmental sustainability. With early detection of factors like pH, moisture, and nutrients, farmers can optimize their practices. This reduces waste, improves crop health, and leads to higher profits with minimal environmental damage.

📈 Benefits of AI-Based Soil Analysis

  • Increases crop productivity
  • Provides data-driven crop suggestions
  • Reduces excessive use of fertilizers and water
  • Supports climate-resilient agriculture
  • Detects early soil degradation or salinity
