🌊 Why Water pH Monitoring Matters

Water pH affects fish health, growth, and survival. Regular monitoring helps prevent stress and disease, ensuring better yield and sustainable aquaculture. Early detection of imbalances allows farmers to take timely action, ensuring optimal water quality, improving yield, and promoting a stable, sustainable aquaculture environment.

📈 Benefits of AI-Based Water pH Monitoring

  • Improves fish health and survival rates
  • Enables real-time water quality insights
  • Reduces disease risks through early detection
  • Optimizes feed usage and pond management
  • Supports sustainable and efficient aquaculture
